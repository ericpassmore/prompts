# Regulatory Surface

## Scope Evaluated

The idea defines an internal engineering process for capturing and improving defects, inefficiencies, and poor-fit behavior in shared agent skills. The process targets issue management, triage, validation, and rollout for developer tooling rather than customer-facing regulated operations.

## Surface Determination

- Status: `NO_SURFACE`
- No domain-specific legal or sectoral regulatory program is directly implicated by this scope as currently defined.
- The process does not itself handle payments, healthcare delivery, consumer lending, employment decisions, export-controlled execution, or regulated records management.

## Rationale

- The evaluated scope is an internal workflow for engineering quality improvement.
- The described actions are issue capture, classification, clustering, and process refinement inside a software repository.
- No user requirement introduced regulated data collection, regulated decisioning, or external compliance obligations specific to this process.

## Residual Watchpoints

- Re-evaluate if the process later introduces persistent collection of personal data from external users, compliance reporting obligations, or policy enforcement with legal effect.
- Re-evaluate if rollout automation starts acting across organizational boundaries with privileged access not already covered by standard repository governance.
